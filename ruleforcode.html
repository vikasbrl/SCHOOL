RULE OF CODE AND CONDUCT

Respect for All

All students must treat their peers, teachers, and staff members with respect. Bullying, discrimination, or harassment of any kind will not be tolerated.

Attendance and Punctuality

Students are expected to attend school regularly and arrive on time. Parents must inform the school in case of absence or late arrival.

Academic Integrity

Students must maintain honesty in all academic activities. Cheating, plagiarism, or any form of dishonest behavior will result in strict consequences.

Dress Code

All students are required to wear the prescribed school uniform and maintain a neat appearance. Casual or inappropriate attire is not permitted.

Use of School Property

Students must handle school property with care. Any damage caused intentionally or due to negligence will require compensation.
